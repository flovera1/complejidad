Doctors in the US do an operation. It changes the life of a baby. The baby's name is Dominique. She is born with four legs and other parts of a body. They are parts of a twin. The twin is on her back. There are legs and feet. They move. This is very dangerous for the baby. Doctors work very hard. The operation is very difficult. It is successful in the end. The baby has a new life. She can live a full and normal life now. Dominique leaves the hospital five days after the operation. She lives in an American family. They are not her parents. They look after her.

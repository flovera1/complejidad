This news is about a snake. The snake lives in a zoo. It is the biggest snake in Europe. It is 23 feet long (7 metres). It weighs around 60 kilograms. The snake gets a check-up. A vet checks the snake's heart. The doctor uses an ultrasound. Experts try similar tests on giraffes and sharks. However, snake hearts interest them more.

People find something amazing in Britain. They find very old human footprints. The footprints are very special. They are the oldest footprints in Europe. Older footprints are only in Africa. Scientists take pictures of the footprints. They use the pictures to make a model. There's a conference in London. The pictures and the model are shown there. Most of the footprints are children's. This is a great discovery. It gives us new information. We will understand history better.

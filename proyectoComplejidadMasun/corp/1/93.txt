This story is about a cat. It lives in Chicago. A woman finds the cat in an alley. The woman takes the cat in. She names the cat Alley. The cat has a special talent. The woman sees this. She helps the cat get better at the talent. In the beginning, the cat can jump two feet. Then it can jump three feet, four feet, five feet and then six feet! The cat is a star. She sets a new Guinness World Record. The cat's owner is a cat trainer.

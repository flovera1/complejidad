In February, a husband and a wife go on a cruise. The wife disappears. Police arrest the husband. They think that he does something to his wife. Now it is March. In Italy, a boat owner is at a port. He sees a suitcase in the water. He picks it up. It is heavy. He unzips the suitcase. He finds a black bag inside. It looks like there is a person in the bag. Italian police think that the wife is in the black bag. Her husband's lawyer says that it is not her body. The police are still working.
